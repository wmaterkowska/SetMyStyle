Declutter your wardrobe
and discover your style —
one piece at a time.

Set{My}Style is your personal wardrobe assistant — designed to help you organize your clothes, declutter with purpose, and uncover your unique style. Add your pieces, reflect on how they make you feel, and discover patterns in what you love (and what you don't).

Download for Android

What you can do

Wardrobe

  • Add clothing items with photos from camera or gallery
  • Describe each piece with colors, patterns, cut, textile, occasion, and more
  • Rate by comfort, fit feel, price, and keep-or-let-go
  • Edit, delete, and filter by any combination of properties

Outfits

  • Build outfits by combining items from your wardrobe
  • Browse and manage saved outfits
  • View outfit details and edit them at any time
  • One item can belong to many outfits

Discoveries

  • Wardrobe insights and statistics
  • See which colors, patterns, and occasions dominate your wardrobe
  • Identify underused items and style tendencies

Summaries

  • Deep analysis by category, feel, frequency, and appearance
  • See which styles and fits you keep vs. let go
  • Discover what you actually wear and what makes you feel like yourself
